DUMPLY ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the DUMPLY mobile application.

01

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
  • Task & Note Data — The tasks, notes, and categories you create within the app.
  • Voice Data — When you use the voice assistant feature, your voice is temporarily processed by Deepgram for speech-to-text conversion. We do not store raw audio recordings.
  • Usage Data — Basic usage statistics such as task completion counts, stored to power your in-app statistics.
02

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the DUMPLY app experience:

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account
  • To store and sync your tasks, notes, and categories across sessions
  • To process voice commands and convert them to actionable tasks or notes
  • To display your personal productivity statistics within the app
We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and row-level security policies to ensure only you can access your data.

Voice input is processed in real-time and is not stored as audio on our servers.
05

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

06

Children's Privacy
