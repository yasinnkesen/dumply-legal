DUMPLY Privacy Policy Your Privacy Matters Last updated: March 12, 2026 DUMPLY ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the DUMPLY mobile application. 1. Information We Collect We collect only the information necessary to provide our services: Account Information — When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. Task & Note Data — The tasks, notes, and categories you create within the app. Voice Data — When you use the voice assistant feature, your voice is temporarily processed by Deepgram (a third-party speech-to-text service) to convert speech to text. We do not store raw audio recordings. Usage Data — Basic usage statistics such as task completion counts, stored to power your in-app statistics. 2. How We Use Your Information Your data is used solely to provide and improve the DUMPLY app experience: To authenticate you and maintain your account To store and sync your tasks, notes, and categories across sessions To process voice commands and convert them to actionable tasks or notes To display your personal productivity statistics within the app We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. 3. Third-Party Services DUMPLY uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies: Supabase — Database and authentication infrastructure. Privacy Policy Google Sign-In — Authentication provider. Privacy Policy Deepgram — Speech-to-text processing for voice commands. Privacy Policy OpenAI — AI processing for understanding voice commands. Privacy Policy 4. Data Storage & Security Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and row-level security policies to ensure only you can access your data. Voice input is processed in real-time and is not stored as audio on our servers. 5. Data Retention Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. 6.
